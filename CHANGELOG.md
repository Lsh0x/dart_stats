## 0.1.0

- Initial release
- Descriptive statistics: mean, variance, standard deviation, standard error, z-score, percentile, median, IQR
- Distributions: Normal, LogNormal, Exponential, Uniform with fitting (MLE)
- Distribution fitting: auto_fit, fit_all, AIC/BIC ranking, Kolmogorov-Smirnov test
- Linear regression: fit, predict, confidence intervals, r-squared
- Hypothesis tests: paired t-test, one-sample t-test
- Utility functions: gamma, beta, erf/erfc, combinatorics
